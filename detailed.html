Egypt

Program :

Day 1:

Arrival in Cairo Arrive at Cairo International Airport. Transfer to your hotel and check-in. Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the local area or relaxing at the hotel.



Day 2:

Pyramids of Giza and Sphinx Visit the iconic Pyramids of Giza, including the Great Pyramid of Khufu. Explore the ancient Sphinx and the surrounding plateau. Optional: Camel ride around the pyramids for a unique perspective.



Day 3:

Egyptian Museum and Khan El Khalili Bazaar Tour the Egyptian Museum in Cairo, home to a vast collection of ancient artifacts, including treasures from Tutankhamun's tomb. Explore Khan El Khalili, one of the oldest markets in the Middle East, known for its vibrant atmosphere and diverse array of shops.



Day 4:

Fly to Luxor, often referred to as the world's greatest open-air museum. Visit the Karnak Temple Complex, dedicated to the gods Amun, Mut, and Khonsu. Explore the Luxor Temple, an ancient Egyptian temple complex located on the east bank of the Nile River.



Day 5:

Discover the Valley of the Kings, the burial ground for many pharaohs of the New Kingdom. Explore the Temple of Hatshepsut, one of the most beautiful and well-preserved temples in Egypt. Visit the Colossi of Memnon, two massive stone statues of the Pharaoh Amenhotep III.



Day 6:

Travel to Aswan, a serene city located on the Nile River. Visit the Philae Temple, dedicated to the goddess Isis, which was relocated to Agilkia Island to save it from being submerged by the Nile. Enjoy a leisurely felucca ride on the Nile, taking in the picturesque scenery.



Day 7:

Optional day trip to Abu Simbel, home to the impressive temples of Ramses II and his wife Nefertari. Marvel at the colossal statues and intricate carvings that adorn the temples. Return to Aswan for overnight stay.



Day 8:

Transfer to Aswan Airport for your departure flight or extend your stay to explore more of Egypt.
